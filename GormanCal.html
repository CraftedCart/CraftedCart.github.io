Gormanian, what's that?

I'm going to guess that you don't watch Dave Gorman Modern Life is Goodish then. The Gormanian calendar is a idea from series 3 episode 7 of the Goodish show. We know how many seconds are in a minute, how many minutes are in an hour, how many hours are in a day, how many days there are in a week, how many months there are in a year, but we don't know how many days or weeks there are in a month. It changes each month. The Gormanian calendar solves this issue by making each month 28 days long, as well as by adding another month and an intermission. This calendar also renames and moves some months. (OCTober is the 10th month, not the 8th?)
